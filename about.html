About TradeTracker

TradeTracker is my own personal project as a way to encapsulate my learning of programming. TradeTracker has developed and grown with me as I began to learn more and more about coding, originally TradeTracker existed as just an HTML file with no styling, I have since incorporated advanced CSS styling using flex-box, and front end Javascript programming which is used for calculations and Dom manipulation. Future plans for TradeTracker include API incorporation to pull real time data as well as projecting graphs for stock trends, furthermore I am looking to add an account system in which users may have a dashboard to show all pinned stocks and to allow for information to be retained between visits.

About Me!

Hi, I am Jacob Vaanholt and I am the founder of TradeTracker, I am 17 years old and have a deep love and drive for programming, TradeTracker is the first website I created and has combined my love of finance and programming into one, I am a firm believer in learning by doing and work on this website at every chance I get.
